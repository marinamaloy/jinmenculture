Booking FAQ

📌 如何预约品牌演出？

📌 How do I book a brand show?

📌 演出可以定制吗？

📌 Can the show be customized?

是的。所有演出都可以根据品牌需求定制内容、时长、互动形式和主题。我们与您合作，打造与您的品牌形象完美契合的独特体验。

Yes. All shows can be customized in content, duration, interaction style, and theme. We work with you to create a unique experience that perfectly aligns with your brand identity.

📌 演出费用是多少？

📌 How much does the show cost?

费用取决于演出形式、时长、演员数量及定制程度。体验场¥3,500起，标准场¥5,500起。我们会在咨询时提供详细报价。

Pricing depends on format, duration, number of performers, and customization. Trial shows start at ¥3,500, Standard shows from ¥5,500. We provide a detailed quote during consultation.

📌 需要提前多久预约？

📌 How far in advance should I book?

建议提前2–4周预约，特别是周末和旺季（Q4及春节前后）。微醺秀等成熟项目可缩短至1周。尽早联系我们以确保档期。

We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and peak season (Q4 and Chinese New Year). For established shows like Weixunxiu, bookings can be confirmed in 1 week. Contact us early to secure your date.
