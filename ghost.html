About the Show

打破传统演出边界，打造现象级城市娱乐新地标
一个为当代年轻消费者量身定制的社交娱乐产品

《马戏团的幽魂》是一项以原创IP故事为核心，深度融合魔术、戏剧、悬疑与沉浸惊魂互动体验的先锋商业娱乐项目。项目旨在为目标客群打造一场集"秀、互动、悬疑、奇情"于一体的90分钟先锋惊魂之旅。

Ghost of the Circus is a pioneering commercial entertainment project with an original IP story at its core, deeply integrating magic, theatre, mystery, and immersive horror interaction. It aims to deliver a 90-minute cutting-edge horror journey combining spectacle, interaction, suspense, and emotion.

我们不仅仅是在上演一场秀，更是在运营一个可复制、高粘性的社交娱乐产品。

We are not just staging a show — we are operating a replicable, highly sticky social entertainment product.

Investment FAQ

📌 我需要投资多少钱才能参与这个项目？

📌 How much do I need to invest to participate?

投资金额为¥400,000–¥560,000，涵盖制作启动成本。我们提供三种合作模式：制作人模式、投资人模式和场地驻场合作，可根据您的需求灵活选择。

Investment ranges from ¥400,000 to ¥560,000, covering production start-up costs. We offer three partnership models: Producer Model, Investor Model, and Venue Residency – flexible options to match your needs.

📌 什么时候能收回投资？

📌 When can I expect to break even?

根据财务模型，预计回本周期为5–12周（按每周8场演出、70%上座率计算）。上海剧场行业标准为15周内回本，我们的项目属于极低风险。

Based on our financial model, the expected break-even period is 5–12 weeks (calculated at 8 shows per week, 70% capacity). Shanghai theatre industry standard is under 15 weeks – our project is considered very low risk.

📌 演出需要多长时间筹备？

📌 How long does it take to prepare the show?

制作周期约2–3个月，包括剧本定稿、幻觉机关安装、演员排练和试演。我们已经完成试演并获得好评，制作流程已经非常成熟。

Production takes approximately 2–3 months, including final script development, illusion installation, cast rehearsals, and preview performances. We've already completed successful preview show with positive reviews.

📌 你们需要什么样的场地？

📌 What kind of venue do you need?

需要200–300㎡的空间，层高4米以上，容纳100–150位观众。适合演艺新空间、商业综合体或剧场。我们提供全套舞美设计和幻觉装置。

We need 200–300㎡ of space with a minimum ceiling height of 4 meters, accommodating 100–150 guests. Suitable for performance spaces, commercial complexes, or theatres. We provide complete stage design and illusion installation.
