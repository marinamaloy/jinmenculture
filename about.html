我们是谁

Who We Are

浸门文化成立于上海，是一家专注于幻觉工程设计与先锋演出的创意技术公司。我们相信：在这个数字过剩的时代，真正能触动人心的，永远是那个让你在现实中无法解释的瞬间。

Jinmen Culture is a Shanghai-based creative technology company specializing in illusion engineering and immersive productions. We believe: in this age of digital excess, what truly moves people is that moment in reality that you simply cannot explain.

从近景魔术到大型沉浸式制作，从商业场馆到品牌活动，我们用12年的行业经验，将"不可能"变成可以被观众亲眼见证的真实体验。我们不是特效公司——我们是用物理手段创造魔法幻觉的工程师。

From close-up magic to large-scale immersive productions, from commercial venues to brand events — we use 12 years of industry experience to turn "impossible" into real experiences that audiences witness with their own eyes. We are not a special effects company — we are engineers who create physical magic.

我们的使命是创造让观众无法解释、无法忘记的瞬间。这些瞬间不需要屏幕，不需要后期处理——只需要一个真实的人，和一个精心设计的幻觉。

Our mission is to create moments that audiences cannot explain and cannot forget. These moments need no screen, no post-production — only a real person and a meticulously designed illusion.

我们的核心理念

Our Values

物理为本

Physical First

在这个数字特效泛滥的时代，我们选择相信亲眼所见。真实的物理魔法，拥有任何屏幕都无法替代的震撼力量。

In an age of infinite digital effects, we choose to believe in what happens right in front of your eyes. Nothing a screen shows you will ever match a real impossibility.

⚙

工程驱动

Engineering Driven

每一个幻觉背后，都是精密的力学计算、安全设计与反复测试。我们的"魔法"是可以被建造师签字的工程作品。

Behind every illusion: precise mechanics, safety engineering, and obsessive testing. Our "magic" is engineering work that a licensed constructor can sign off on.

情感至上

Emotion Above All

技术只是手段。我们真正在意的，是那个让观众暂时失去语言能力的瞬间。那才是我们的产品。

Technology is only the means. What we truly care about is the moment when an audience member loses the ability to speak. That is our product.
